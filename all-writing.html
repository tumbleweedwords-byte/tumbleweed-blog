Fiction · Berlin
train in vain | short fiction

“I wait for a train that circles the city like bats. At night in Berlin you can imagine anything you want. The carriages are full of inviting people I never talk to.”

Written on a Berlin U-Bahn in winter. A punk on the platform, chewing gum the colour of her hair. A toothless man watching her move. The Clash in your ears because some sounds were born to travel.
