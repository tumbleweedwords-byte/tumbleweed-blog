About Tumbleweed Words

David (me) is an internationally published champion of fiction and poetry.

Read the newsletter → Explore Fiction & Poetry

Original fiction and poetry here there and everywhere. Hang out and get into the arts from a bunch of different angles.

Nomadic and sporadic international based and published, fiction and poetry

My fiction and poetry have been published internationally in New York, San Francisco, Lisbon, Toronto, Philadelphia, Dundee, Edinburgh, London, Oxford, Buenos Aires. I’ve been shortlisted for the Pushcart Prize one time. Tumbleweed Words has been on Substack for over five years and has gained 1,200+ global subscribers organically. The best place to start is probably my best of collection, but you decide that as reader.

Be in touch, reach out

The best way to reach me is through Substack, where I read all replies to the newsletter. Every email gets read. If you write literary fiction, flash fiction, or poetry and want to connect, reply to any post.
